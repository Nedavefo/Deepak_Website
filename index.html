Our Vision and Mission

What We Do For You

Vision Statement

To become the leading platform that bridges households and retailers, fostering a vibrant community where both benefit from meaningful discounts, incentives, and rewards. We aim to create a world where every grocery purchase becomes a smart investment, empowering customers to save while supporting retailers in building lasting relationships.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to connect household customers with retail partners through an innovative savings platform that transforms everyday shopping into a rewarding experience. By offering attractive discounts, incentives, and cash-back opportunities, we strive to build an active and healthy network of customers and retailers, ensuring mutual growth and sustained benefits for both parties.

About URS Daily Savings

Know Who We Are

Leading the Way in Connecting Consumers and Retailers

At URS Daily Savings, we’re a dedicated team of professionals focused on creating value for households and retailers alike. Our unique model functions like bank dividends, delivering shared benefits that strengthen the relationship between consumers and retail partners. By connecting consumers and retailers in a mutually rewarding way, our solution fosters growth for both.

Since 2020, we’ve been developing this innovative approach, and we're excited to bring it to life this year, helping meet both domestic needs and business objectives for everyone’s success.
